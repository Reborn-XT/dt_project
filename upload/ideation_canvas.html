Sheet No: 7
Participants: Uday M. & Samarth T.
Project Title: "The Derma Project"
Target Audience
Dermatology Patients
Clinic Receptionists
Consulting Dermatologists
Local Pharmacists
Situation
  • Booking an appointment from home with high wait times
  • Waiting at the crowded clinic reception area
  • Doctor reviewing past patient history from disorganized physical files
  • Patient trying to track their skin healing progress over weeks
  • Patient buying prescribed branded medicines at a tied-up pharmacy
  • Patient feeling financial strain and looking for cheaper generic medicine alternatives
Solution / Features
  • Digital appointment booking calendar (Smart Booking)
  • Cloud-based patient history database for easy access
  • Built-in skin condition tracker with photo timeline overlays
  • Database mapping expensive brand prescriptions to cheap generic options
  • Automated follow-up and medicine schedule notifications
  • Accessible mobile-first interface designed for all age groups
