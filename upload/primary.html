CURRENT STATUS OF OPERATIONS

Booking Experience

  • Approximately 68% of patients reported confusion with their appointment scheduling.
  • The reliance on a handwritten register leads to missing dates.
Inference:
The current manual registration system is outdated and prone to errors.

THE IMPACT ON PATIENTS

Medication Costs

  • 75% of patients spend over ₹1500 monthly on branded medicines.
  • 75% of patients are completely unaware of generic alternatives.
Inference:
High costs create a huge financial burden, exacerbated by lack of education.

VALIDATION OF SOLUTION

Acceptance of Digitization

  • 83% of respondents expressed strong interest in a digital app.
  • 90% stated that a generic medicine suggestion tool would be helpful.
Conclusion:
The survey strongly validates the need for a digital, educational ecosystem.
